To make this a scalable SaaS product, you should think of it as three separate
applications sharing one backend, rather than a single app.
1. Pinesphere Admin Portal (Super Admin)
2. Property Owner App
3. Guest Portal (via WhatsApp/PWA)
This gives Pinesphere complete control over every customer, subscription, and
property.

Non-Payment Flow
Instead of immediately deleting the property, use a controlled lifecycle.
Day 1
•

Subscription expires

•

Warning shown

•

All features continue

Day 7
•

Grace period ends

•

New bookings disabled

•

Check-in disabled

•

Existing guests can still check out

•

Reports remain available

•

Data export remains available

Day 30
•

Property becomes suspended

•

Offline token expires

•

App enters read-only mode

Day 60–90
•

Archive property

•

Notify customer before archival

•

Retain data for the configured retention period

After retention period
•

Permanently delete only after multiple reminders and according to your terms of
service.

This approach protects customer trust while still enforcing subscriptions.
