‚óè‚Äã Grace period after expiry: After expiry, the owner is given 5 bonus days to pay
before enforcement kicks in.

3.6 Payment Delivery ‚Äî WhatsApp QR
‚óè‚Äã When a renewal is due, Pinesphere sends the payment QR code directly through
WhatsApp to the registered number.
‚óè‚Äã Owner scans/opens the QR, completes payment online (UPI/cards/net banking), and
the cloud is updated immediately.
‚óè‚Äã Once the device reconnects, the new license token is downloaded and the offline app
is updated automatically ‚Äî no manual re-entry needed.

3.7 Relationship to the Offline License Engine
‚óè‚Äã Every property holds a locally stored, digitally signed license token (License ID,
Property ID, Expiry Date, Features, Device Count).
‚óè‚Äã Subscription Management (this module) is the control plane; the license token is the
offline enforcement mechanism the app checks locally without needing to call the
server on every login.
